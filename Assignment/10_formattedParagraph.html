HTML elements are the building blocks of HTML Pages.With HTML constructs, images and other objects such as interactive forms may be embedded into the rendered page.HTML provides a meansx to create structured documents by denoting structural semantics for text such as headings, paragraphs,lists,links, quotes and other items. HTML elements are delineated by tags,written using angel brackets.Tags such as <img/> and <input/> directly introduce content into the page.Other tags such as <p> surround and provide information about document text and may include other tags as sub-elements.Browser do not display the HTML tags but use them to interpret the content of the page.
